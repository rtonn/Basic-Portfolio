About Me

I was responsible for the electronic data exchange processes between colleges, universities, lenders, servicers, the department of education, and our 3rd party vendors. I have a good ability to connect with people. In my last job, I was recognized for my number of customer relationships which resulted in a good reputation for our company, which ultimately increased our business.
